**Status:** Spec for production  
**Placement:** After `reveal-stakes`  
**Stable step id:** `reveal-diagnosis`

Culmination beat — personalized diagnosis, manageable gap, retake confidence, one-paragraph “why we’re different.” Reframe ability → optimization.

- [ ] Headline: score gap looks fixable (pronoun-aware)

- [ ] Gap + timeline: `{gap_pts}` to `{target}` — manageable framing
- [ ] Retake confidence when `test_date` or history supports it
- [ ] One short “why different” paragraph (convergence, not lecture)
- [ ] Continue → `reveal-bottlenecks`
